Hash Brown Recipe

Description

Hash browns are traditionally potatoes shredded down and fried in oil to give it a crunch.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • egg
  • oil
  • potatoes

Steps

  1. Shred your potatoes with your prefered method.
  2. Rinse the potatoes until the water is clear and then squeeze out any remaining water.
  3. Mix the egg and flour with the potatoe shreds throughly as you heat up some oil in a pan.
  4. Separate the mix into patties and place in oil, on each side, for five minutes.
