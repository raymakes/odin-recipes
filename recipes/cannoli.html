Cannoli Recipe

Description

Cannoli is a traditional recipe that was created in Sicily. The outer shell is made up of a pastry while the inside filling can be made up of various mixes (usually cheese and vanilla). Some like to add topping on the openings.

Ingredients

  • pre-made outer shells
  • ricotta cheese
  • powdered sugar
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Get out a bowl and put two cups of ricotta cheese in it.
  2. Add sugar and mix as needed until desired sweetness.
  3. Add a splash of vanilla extract and mix. Add more if needed.
  4. Spoon the mixture carefully into the shell openning with a small sugar spoon until filled.
